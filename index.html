About QueueEase

QueueEase is a branch management system developed by SimpleSolutions

QueueEase offers customers unparalleled convenience by enabling online queuing and appointment scheduling, reducing wait times, and providing flexibility in selecting services and branches, enhancing the overall customer experience.

QueueEase empowers banks with efficient queue and appointment management, optimizing branch operations. It enhances customer satisfaction, streamlines staff tasks, and provides data-driven insights for better decision-making.

Mobile

The mobile side of the system caters to the bank's customers, providing them with the convenience of queuing online and scheduling appointments from the comfort of their homes or while at the branch. Customers have the flexibility to scan their presence when physically present at the branch or join a queue remotely at the nearest branch offering their desired service. Furthermore, customers can easily schedule appointments at their preferred branch or opt for the nearest branch available at the time of appointment booking. Customers can provide feedback and customer experience via reviews.

Web Assistant

The assistant side of the system is tailored for branch assistants, empowering them to efficiently oversee and handle the queue and customer appointments. Assistants can access their allocated schedule and also the work assigned. Assistants wield the authority to approve or decline customer-made appointments, ensuring flexibility and control in the scheduling process. Additionally, they manage the queue by attending to individuals waiting in line, maintaining order and efficiency. Assistants can access comprehensive performance data, including leaderboards specific to their branch, and review feedback from customers they've assisted, offering valuable insights for continuous improvement.

Web Management

The management side of the system is designed for managers who play a pivotal role in system administration. Managers have the authority to schedule assistants and allocate specific counters for their assignments. They possess the capability to monitor the system's performance, with data available on hourly, weekly, monthly, and yearly metrics, enabling data-driven decision-making. Managers can further expand the system's functionality by adding or removing assistants, incorporating new managers into the system, and introducing additional services offered by the branch. This comprehensive control ensures effective management of the system, optimizing its performance and adaptability.

Technologies Used In Project

Java

QueueEase utilizes Java for its core backend functionality. Java's versatility and reliability make it the ideal choice for handling complex server-side tasks efficiently.

Spring-boot Framework

The Spring Boot Framework empowers QueueEase with rapid development capabilities. It simplifies the setup and development of robust, production-ready applications.

MySql Database

QueueEase relies on MySQL to manage and store its data securely. MySQL's scalability and performance ensure seamless data management for the application.

C# Mobile

For mobile app development, QueueEase leverages C#. This versatile language provides the foundation for creating feature-rich and user-friendly mobile experiences.

Xamarin

Xamarin is instrumental in crafting cross-platform mobile apps for QueueEase. It enables consistent and high-performance app delivery across various mobile platforms.

JavaScript

JavaScript enhances the interactivity and responsiveness of QueueEase's user interface. It plays a vital role in creating a dynamic and user-friendly web experience.

CSS

CSS is employed to style and design QueueEase's web interface. It ensures a visually appealing and consistent user interface across different devices and browsers.

HTML

HTML forms the backbone of QueueEase's web pages. It structures content and ensures compatibility across web browsers, making the application accessible to a broad audience.

Book Appointment

Book Appointment Page Description

Users can conveniently book appointments at a branch using two methods: by searching for their desired branch or by utilizing their current location. The available services for selection are context-sensitive, ensuring users are presented with choices based on whether they choose a specific branch or use their location. Queue Status

Queue Status Page Description

The Queue Status page provides real-time updates to users regarding their position in the queue. In the initial frame, when the user is awaiting their turn, the position indicator typically shows a value greater than 0, which corresponds to the number of people in the queue ahead of them. In this specific scenario, where only the user is in the queue, the position is set to 1, representing their position.

The second frame indicates a shift in the position, with the value transitioning to 0. This signifies that the user is now next in line to receive service. Clear instructions guide the user to proceed to the counter where the designated assistant is stationed for their service. During this phase, the option to cancel the queue is intentionally disabled, ensuring a smooth and efficient process for users as they proceed to be served by the assistant. This approach enhances the overall user experience within the system.

Dashboard Description

The Manager Dashboard is a comprehensive tool tailored for managers, providing valuable insights into branch operations. Managers can seamlessly track hourly queue trends within the branch, and they have the flexibility to filter these trends for previous days, enabling data-driven decision-making. The hourly trend chart further empowers users to specify the number of previous days to make predictive assessments of daily queue activity, aiding in identifying peak times of high traffic.

Assistant Dashboard
Assistant Dashboard

Assistant Dashboard

Assistant Dashboard Description

The assistant dashboard in QueueEase provides a comprehensive overview of an assistant's responsibilities and performance. It includes a list of appointments that have been approved by the assistant, and the assistant has the capability to mark appointments as "done" once they have been completed. Additionally, assistants can access their daily schedule, which includes start-of-day, break times, and end-of-day schedules. These schedules are assigned by managers to ensure a smooth workflow.

Furthermore, the assistant dashboard features a star rating system that categorizes ratings by the number of stars awarded by customers. These ratings are visualized on a chart, allowing assistants to track their performance over time. Assistants can also view the performance of other assistants by the number of queues served, and they are ranked accordingly based on their performance, fostering healthy competition and continuous improvement among the team.

Management of Queue
Queue Management

Queue Management

Queue Management Description

Within QueueEase, when customers join the queue via their smartphones, it becomes essential for assistants to manage and process these queues efficiently. Assistants have visibility into the queue, enabling them to identify the next person in line who requires service. They can then call that queue and provide assistance to the individual, ensuring a streamlined and organized service delivery process.

Additionally, assistants possess the authority to cancel a queue if the person fails to show up after being called. This feature helps maintain queue integrity and optimizes the use of resources, ensuring a smooth flow of customers through the system.
